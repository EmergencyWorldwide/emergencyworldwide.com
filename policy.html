Emergency Dispatch Simulator - Game Policy & Terms of Service

Game Policy

1. Gameplay Guidelines

  • The simulator is designed for educational and entertainment purposes only
  • Players must manage emergency resources responsibly within the game
  • Strategic placement of emergency facilities is encouraged for optimal response times
  • Players should prioritize emergency responses based on severity and available resources

2. Resource Management

  • Players start with a fixed amount of funds for building facilities and purchasing units
  • Additional funds are earned through successful mission completions
  • Resource allocation should be balanced between different emergency service types
  • Proper maintenance of emergency response units is required

3. Fair Play

  • Exploitation of game mechanics or bugs is prohibited
  • Players should not intentionally cause system performance issues
  • Sharing of strategies and tips is encouraged within the community
  • Respect for other players and emergency services is mandatory
